How we got here

Built on a simple idea.

Most insurance agents win when you lose. When a carrier jacks your premium up 30%, the agent's commission goes up 30% with it — so renewals tend to happen on autopilot. We opened Intac in 2004 to do the opposite.

Independent from day one, we shop your policy across 40+ carriers every year. The one that gave you the best rate last year might be the one jacking it up this year — and Texas is one of the hardest states in the country to insure, with rates that can swing 30% overnight. We catch that before renewal, not after.

Twenty‑two years later, we've handled claims through hurricanes, ice storms, hailstorms, and at least one truly spectacular kitchen fire. We've watched clients' kids grow up, buy their first homes, start businesses. We've insured all of it — because that's the fun part of the job.

What we believe

Three things we never compromise on.

01

Not commission‑focused

Most agents earn more when your rate goes up — so they let policies renew on autopilot. We're the first agency built to do the opposite. Your premium drops, our commission drops. That's the deal.

02

Hundreds of markets

A captive Allstate agent can only shop Allstate. We shop 40+ carriers — and re‑quote every policy every year. Texas rates swing 30% overnight, and we catch which carriers want your business this year and which don't.

03

A human answers

Every client has a named agent. When you call, text, or email, you reach a person who knows your policy. When a claim hits — evenings, weekends, during hurricanes — we pick up.
