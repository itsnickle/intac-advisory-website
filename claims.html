What to do first

Four steps. In this order.

Whether it's a fender‑bender or a kitchen fire, the right first moves protect you and make the claim smoother.

Make sure everyone's safe

Call 911 if there's any injury. Move to a safe location. Nothing matters more than this step.

Document everything

Photos of damage, other vehicles, license plates, the scene. Names and contact info of anyone involved or witnessing.

Call us

We'll walk you through what to file, what your deductible is, and what to expect. We'll also file the claim with your carrier so you don't have to.

Follow up with the adjuster

Once your carrier assigns an adjuster, we stay on the call with you throughout — and escalate if things stall.
